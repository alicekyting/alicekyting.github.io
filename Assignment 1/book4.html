Icebreaker

"Icebreaker" by Alex Berman is a practical guide for salespeople on how to successfully reach out to potential clients and start conversations.
The book is focused on providing readers with a clear and actionable framework for generating leads and building relationships with prospects.
I enjoy this book is beacuse the writer provides strategies for finding and targeting ideal prospects, and outlines specific techniques for reaching out to them in a way that is tailored to our interests and needs.
