Six of Crows

"Six of Crows" by Leigh Bardugo is a captivating fantasy novel that follows a group of six misfits on an impossible heist.
This book features a richly drawn world with intricate politics and cultural details, and an intricate and suspenseful plot that keeps readers guessing.
The well-developed and complex characters, particularly Kaz Brekker, the leader of the group, add an extra layer of depth and intrigue to the story.

Overall, "Six of Crows" is a must-read for fans of the fantasy genre, particularly those who enjoy heist stories and epic adventures.
