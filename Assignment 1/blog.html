My Book List

Shadow and Bone

Author : Leigh Bardugo

Shadow and Bone is a popular young adult fantasy novel by Leigh Bardugo set in the fictional world of Ravka. The story follows Alina Starkov, an orphan and mapmaker who discovers she has the rare ability to summon light. She is taken to a magical academy to train as a Grisha, a group of people with magical abilities who serve the kingdom. However, as Alina tries to master her powers, she becomes embroiled in a dangerous political plot involving the power-hungry Darkling, who seeks to use her powers for his own gain.
